11/14 Sngular USA Fall Event

Let's get together for a collaborative contest across several online challenges.

What is it about?

Get together for team collaboration in a puzzle-race based contest. All teams will work on the same goal, solving online challenges on the way. Each team will be scored based on their progress. Be amongst the teams with the highest score to win a prize! The event will be held on November 14 from 10:00 am to 6:00 pm. Puzzles will be based on different aspects, ranging from technical to social.

How do I sign up?

Save the date and register on the form below. Chat with other Sngulars to arrange teams of 3 or 4 members. All team members need to specify the same team name on the form. If you want to participate but not sure who to team up with, we have you covered! Just leave it blank and we'll find the best team for you. After signing up for the event, stay tuned you'll receive communications on your Sngular email.

How can I know more?

In case you need any clarification on the event, reach out to your Sngular manager. They will be able to help you out, answer frequently asked questions, or communicate any other questions to the event committee.

If you want to speak to us directly, send an email out to hello@prticular.io.
